Critical Issues

Contact Form Present ✓

Your contact page has a working web form (Name, Email, Message). Consider adding a dedicated "Request a Free Quote" form with service type and address fields to improve lead quality and enable automated routing.

Strength — Can Be Enhanced

Zero Structured Data (JSON-LD) on Any Page

None of your 21 pages have LocalBusiness, Service, BreadcrumbList, or FAQ schema markup. This means Google and AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity) have no structured way to understand your business. You're invisible to rich results and AI overviews.

High Impact — Invisible to AI Search

No Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, or Accessibility Statement

All three legally required pages are missing. This is an ADA compliance risk and a Google trust signal gap. If you run any tracking (GA4, analytics), you're also violating CCPA/GDPR requirements.

High Impact — Legal Liability

No Review or Testimonial Content

There is zero social proof on the entire site. No customer reviews, no testimonials, no star ratings. For a service business, trust is the #1 conversion factor. Without it, visitors leave.

High Impact — Low Trust

No Breadcrumbs (Visual or Schema)

No visible breadcrumb navigation and no BreadcrumbList JSON-LD. Google uses breadcrumbs to understand site hierarchy and display them in search results.

High Impact — Poor Site Structure Signal

Duplicate Meta Titles Across Pages

The About page and Homepage share identical OG titles. Each page must have a unique, keyword-targeted title to rank independently.

High Impact — Cannibalized Rankings

Content Duplication on About Page

The About page renders the same bullet list twice — once as a proper list, then again as plain text. This is duplicate content within a single page and looks unprofessional.

Medium Impact — Quality Signal

Thin Content on Service Pages

Most service pages have only 120-200 words. Google considers pages under 300 words "thin content" and deprioritizes them. Your competitors likely have 500-1000+ words per service page.

Medium Impact — Weak Ranking Signal

No Before/After Photo Gallery

Pressure washing is one of the most visual services. Before/after photos are the single most compelling conversion tool for this industry, and you have none.

Medium Impact — Missed Conversion
